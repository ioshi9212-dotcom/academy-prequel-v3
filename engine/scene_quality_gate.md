# Scene Quality Gate

No stubs. No empty header. No technical preface in play mode.

A meaningful scene must include: visible setting, Akira's visible state, at least one NPC/world reaction, pressure or consequence, and a real choice/reply/risk.

The header must not consume the answer. If the response is short, cut header/options first and keep scene prose.

Reject/avoid these failures:
- invented clothing or props not present in state/current_frame;
- technical numeric option ratings in play output;
- broken Russian phrases or impossible wording;
- NPC actions that contradict the visible scene;
- metadata-only answer without actual scene body.

Avoid repetitive filler and abstract decorative prose.
